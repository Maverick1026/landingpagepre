About Us

Named after the owner's son, Summer, our cafe is located at the prestigious address of 2F Gloriana Bella Building, South Poblacion, Valencia, Negros Oriental.With roots tracing back to the owner's family, this special place, once owned by their grandmother, has been a gathering spot for generations, fostering childhood memories and offering a breathtaking view of the plaza and its surroundings.

In 2008, an entrepreneurial journey began, leading to the establishment of a vibrant restobar that flourished for five years. Now, the vision is to recreate that enchantment by transforming it into a cafe, providing a refined and professional experience. At Summerhouse Cafe, enjoy quality coffee, delightful treats, and a welcoming atmosphere that encourages a sense of community and shared moments, reminiscent of the summerhouse itself.
